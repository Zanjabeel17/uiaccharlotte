Our Story

In 2016, a group of believers from West Africa came together to establish a Masjid for the community. After the money was raised, they put a downpayment on a house. The house has been worked on for many years and the property has been fully paid off thanks to the help of the wonderful community here at Masjid Al-Qiblatain. It is a small community for now, but with the help of Allah, we wish to continue to grow and thrive inshAllah.
